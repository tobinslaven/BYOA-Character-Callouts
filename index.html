Questions & Answers

What is a Character-Callout?

A Character-Callout is a fun way to tell everyone about someone awesome! It's like writing a nice note about what makes a person special.

How do I write a Character-Callout?

It's easy! Just fill out the form on our "Write a Callout" page. Tell us who the person is, why they're awesome, and what words describe them best.

Can I use more than one word to describe someone?

Yes! You can add as many words as you want. Just click the "+ Add another word" button to include more describing words.

Will people know I wrote the callout?

Yes! We include your name so the person knows who wrote the nice things about them. This makes it extra special!

What makes a callout popular?

Popular callouts are the ones that lots of people have read and liked. The more people who see your callout, the more popular it becomes!
